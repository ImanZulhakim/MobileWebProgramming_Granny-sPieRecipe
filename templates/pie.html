How to Make a Delicious Pudding Peach Tart

Looking for a simple and delicious dessert recipe? Try making a pudding peach tart! With just a few ingredients and easy steps, you can create a mouthwatering tart that will impress your friends and family.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 package of pudding mix
  • 3 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1 can of peaches
  • 1 sheet of puff pastry
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 tablespoon of milk
  • Chopped almonds (optional)
  • Powdered sugar (for dusting)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix together 1 cup of milk and the pudding mix until well combined.
  2. Add 3 tablespoons of sugar to the milk mixture and stir until the sugar dissolves.
  3. In a saucepan, heat the milk mixture over medium heat, stirring constantly.
  4. Once the milk is warm, pour in the sugar and pudding mixture.
  5. Cook the mixture for about 1 minute, continuing to stir.
  6. Remove the mixture from the heat and set aside.
  7. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  8. Roll out the puff pastry and place it in a tart pan.
  9. Pour the pudding mixture onto the puff pastry.
  10. Top with a second sheet of puff pastry.
  11. In a small bowl, whisk together the egg yolk and 1 tablespoon of milk.
  12. Brush the egg wash over the top of the tart.
  13. Sprinkle some chopped almonds on top for added crunch (optional).
  14. Bake the tart in the preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until golden brown.
  15. Remove the tart from the oven and let it cool.
  16. In a blender, puree the remaining peaches until smooth.
  17. Spread the peach puree over the cooled tart.
  18. Dust the tart with powdered sugar before serving.
